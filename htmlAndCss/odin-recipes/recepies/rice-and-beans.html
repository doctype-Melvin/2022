Rice and beans

Description

A super-yummy and simple vegan dish.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Canned Beans
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Optional: Scallions

Steps

  1. Cook rice in a pot
  2. Dice garlic and Onion
  3. Heat up frying pan with some oil
  4. Glaze onions in pan
  5. Add garlic to pan
  6. Add cooked rice
  7. When rice starts getting crispy, add drained beans
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste and garnish with scallions
