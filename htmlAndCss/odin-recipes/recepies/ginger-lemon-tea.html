Protein Yoghurt

Description

This is a classic and simple immunesystem booster.

Ingredients

  • Ginger
  • Lemon
  • Water
  • Optional: Honey

Steps

  1. Take a thumb-sized piece of ginger, peel it and cut it into slices
  2. Slice half a Lemon
  3. Put lemon and ginger into a small pot with water
  4. Put a lid on the pot and bring to a boil
  5. Let the tea boil for a minute and then turn down the heat
  6. The tea should simmer for another 10 minutes
  7. Enjoy your tea with a teaspoon of honey
