Protein Yoghurt

Description

This yoghurt is super quick, super cheap and super anabolic.

Ingredients

  • Curd Cheese
  • Protein Powder
  • Water
  • Optional: Sugar-free Sirup

Steps

  1. Put 1 Cup of Curd Cheese into a bowl
  2. Add two tablespoons of water
  3. Mix cheese with water until Yoghurt-like-consistency (add water if needed>
  4. Now add one serving of Protein Powder and mix
  5. For your sweet tooth, add sugar-free sirup
